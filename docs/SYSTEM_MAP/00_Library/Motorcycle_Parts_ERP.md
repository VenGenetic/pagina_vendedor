# Motorcycle Parts ERP

## Description
The **Motorcycle Parts ERP** is the central system for managing the business operations of a motorcycle spare parts vendor. It acts as the single source of truth for inventory tracking, financial recording, and customer management.

## Hierarchy
- **Parent**: None (Root)
- **Children**:
    - [[Inventory_Management]]
    - [[Financial_Management]]

## Core Responsibilities
1.  **Inventory Control**: Tracking stock levels, product details, and movements.
2.  **Financial Ledger**: Recording income, expenses, and account balances via double-entry principles.
3.  **Sales & Customers**: Managing the point-of-sale and customer database.
